Features

Everything You Need to Run
Your Properties

From bookings to payouts, ResIQ handles the entire lifecycle of vacation rental management.

📅

Smart Reservations

Kanban board and table views. Drag-and-drop status management. Multi-property calendar with availability tracking.

💳

Payment Tracking

Track payments by method (Cash, UPI, Gateway, Bank Transfer). Smart collection reminders and financial health scoring.

👤

Guest KYC Portal

Self-service guest portal for ID verification and document upload. QR code check-in for a contactless experience.

📈

Business Analytics

Revenue trends, occupancy heatmaps, channel performance, and ADR/RevPAN analysis across all your properties.

💰

Owner Settlements

Dedicated owner portal with revenue tracking, expense management, and automated payout calculations.

💬

WhatsApp Integration

Send booking confirmations, payment reminders, and check-in details directly via WhatsApp with smart templates.

🤖

AI-Powered Insights

Smart suggestions for pricing, guest follow-ups, and anomaly detection. Rule-based intelligence with zero API costs.

📶

Works Offline

Full PWA with offline support. Install on any device. Background sync ensures no data is ever lost.

📅

OTA Calendar Sync

Sync availability with Airbnb, Booking.com and other OTAs via iCal. Prevent double bookings automatically.
