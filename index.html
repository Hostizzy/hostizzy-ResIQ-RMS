What is ResIQ

Not a PMS. Your Daily
Operations Dashboard.

ResIQ handles what your team does every single day — managing reservations, chasing payments, verifying guests, and staying on top of operations. It's the staff-facing tool that keeps your properties running.

1

Enquiry Comes In

Phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, walk-in — capture every lead with source tracking and follow-up reminders.

2

Create Reservation

Convert enquiries to bookings. Kanban pipeline or table view. Auto-sync with Airbnb & Booking.com via iCal.

3

Collect Payments

Track Cash, UPI, Gateway, Bank Transfer. Smart reminders for pending balances. Full financial health view.

4

Guest Check-in & KYC

Guests upload Aadhar/Passport via QR portal. Selfie verification. Admin review. Meal preferences collected.

5

Communicate & Complete

WhatsApp confirmations, email reminders, check-out thank you messages. Everything templated and tracked.

Features

Everything Your Operations
Team Needs Daily

Built for the team that runs your properties day-to-day. No bloat, no complexity — just the tools that matter.

Reservation Pipeline

Kanban board and table views with drag-and-drop. Filter by property, status, dates, payment. Auto status updates on check-in/check-out dates.

Payment Tracking

Track by method (Cash, UPI, Gateway, Bank Transfer). Outstanding balance alerts. Collection reminders. Full receivable vs collected breakdown.

Guest KYC & Documents

Self-service QR portal for Aadhar, Passport, DL upload with selfie. Admin review & approval workflow. Auto-delete after 60 days.

WhatsApp & Email

Send booking confirmations, payment reminders, check-in instructions via WhatsApp & Gmail. Editable templates with smart placeholders.

Enquiry & Lead Pipeline

Capture leads from every source. Kanban board: New → Contacted → Follow Up → Negotiation → Converted. Priority levels and follow-up tracking.

Business Analytics

Revenue trends, occupancy by property, channel performance (OTA vs Direct), booking type breakdown. Cross-property leaderboard.

OTA Calendar Sync

iCal sync with Airbnb, Booking.com and other OTAs. Prevent double bookings. Manual date blocking. Auto-sync intervals.

Smart Automation

Auto check-in/check-out based on dates. Payment due reminders. Overdue follow-up alerts. Configurable automation intervals.

Works Offline (PWA)

Install on any device. Full offline support with background sync. No data lost even without internet. 95+ Lighthouse score.

OTA Email Import

Import reservations from Gmail. Supports Airbnb, Booking.com, Goibibo, MMT & Agoda. Smart duplicate detection and review.

Owner Portal & Payouts

Dedicated dashboard for property owners. Commission tracking, monthly settlements, payout requests, and expense management.

Financial System

Host payouts, P&L reports, expense tracking per property. Commission auto-calculation with GST support.

Technology & Security

Built on Enterprise-Grade
Infrastructure

Your data is secured by the same providers that power the world's largest applications.

Supabase

PostgreSQL database with real-time subscriptions, row-level security, and automatic backups.

Firebase Auth

Google-grade authentication with secure email/password login, token management, and session handling.

Vercel

Edge-deployed globally for instant load times. Automatic SSL, DDoS protection, and 99.99% uptime SLA.

PWA Technology

Progressive Web App with service workers for offline support, background sync, and native app experience.

Security & Data Protection

End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Tokens encrypted at rest in Supabase with row-level security policies.

Role-Based Access Control

Separate access levels for Admin, Manager, Staff, Owners, and Guests. Each role sees only what they need.

Auto Data Cleanup

Guest KYC documents and personal data automatically deleted 60 days after checkout. Privacy by design.

Token-Based Sessions

Firebase authentication with 24-hour guest session expiry. Server-side token validation on every API call.
